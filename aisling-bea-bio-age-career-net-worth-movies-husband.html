Aisling Cliodhnadh O’Sullivan popularly known as Aisling Bea was born on March 16, 1984 in County Kildare, Ireland to her parents Brian and Helen O’Sullivan. Sinead, her younger sister, is her name. Aisling Bea Career

In 2012, Bea made her television debut with a guest appearance on the BBC comedy series “Holby City.” She went on to appear in a number of other British TV shows, including “The Delivery Man” and “Dead Boss,” before landing a leading role in the acclaimed Channel 4 comedy “The Fall and Rise of Reginald Perrin.”

In 2014, the actress was named one of the “faces of the future” by The Sunday Times, and the following year she won the prestigious Edinburgh Comedy Award for her stand-up show “Plan Bea.” She has also written and starred in her own comedy series, “This Way Up,” which premiered on Channel 4 in 2019 and was later picked up by Hulu in the United States.

Despite her success, she has not shied away from using her platform to address important social issues, including mental health and gender equality. In 2019, she gave a powerful TED Talk on the topic of grief, discussing her experiences coping with the loss of her father.
