How Did Tristian Ranson Die? Founder of the Pet Spa at Laguna Passed Away

Tristian Ranson, founder of the Pet Spa at Laguna and winner of HBO Max’s “Haute Dog” TV series, tragically passed away on Sunday, March 5th, 2023. The news of his sudden and untimely death came as a shock to many of his fans, friends, and colleagues in the pet grooming industry. The news has left a void in the hearts of those who knew him, but his legacy will continue to inspire and motivate others in the pet grooming industry for years to come.

Keep reading to know more about Tristian Ranson, including who he was, how he died, what was the cause of his death, tributes for him, and much more!

Who Was Tristian Ranson?

Tristian Ranson was a well-known figure in the pet grooming industry, renowned for his exceptional skills and creativity. He founded the Pet Spa at Laguna, a luxury pet grooming salon that catered to some of the most pampered pets in California. The salon offered a range of services, from simple baths and haircuts to custom-designed spa treatments and exclusive VIP packages.

Ranson’s talent and expertise in pet grooming earned him a place on HBO Max’s “Haute Dog” TV series. The show followed a group of talented pet groomers as they competed in a series of challenges, showcasing their skills and creativity in front of a panel of judges. Ranson was one of the winners of the show’s first season, earning him widespread recognition and admiration from pet lovers around the world.

He invented his go-to tool, the Andis Pulse ZR II, to produce his jaw-dropping cuts, giving his furry clients a little extra flair in the process. Tristian was the kind of person everyone wishes they could have known because he had a heart of gold. He embodied kindness in every way and the optimism and radiance he had are unmatched.

How Did Tristian Ranson Die? Cause of Death

Tristian Ranson, who was the winner of HBO Max’s “Haute Dog” TV series, sadly and tragically passed away on Sunday, March 5th, 2023. Reportedly, Ranson’s life was cut short in a fatal car accident.

Details about the accident are still scarce, but it is believed that Ranson was driving his car when he collided with another vehicle. The impact of the crash was severe, and Ranson was pronounced dead at the scene.

Ranson may be left us, but his, legacy will live on through the many pets he groomed, the people he inspired, and the impact he had on the pet grooming industry as a whole.

Bardel Bows – Dog Grooming Bows announced Tristian’s passing through the live video on Facebook, saying
