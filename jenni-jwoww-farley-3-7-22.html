Fans Think Jenni 'JWoww' Farley is Pregnant Again

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  389 words  |  Kelle Repass

Jenni ‘JWoww’ Farley got engaged to her boyfriend Zack Carpinello last February, but the two have yet to tie the knot.

They claim that they are “finally” starting to put together some wedding plans since the course of the pandemic seems to finally be slowing, so it’s only a matter of time before they make it official!

But not being married doesn’t mean they can’t start thinking about having more children, and some fans are convinced JWoww might be expecting again…

Credit: ANGELA WEISS/AFP/Getty Images

According to The Sun, some fans are convinced Jenni ‘JWoww’ Farley is pregnant.

They claim they’ve spotted a baby bump on the Jersey Shore star while she was out partying at a club. Of course, that might not be the best place for a pregnant woman, but she could have just been drinking water!

JWoww was wearing a body-con dress, and fans were immediately drawn to her midsection.

The reality star is currently engaged to Zack Clayton Carpinello, so it is not out of the question that they could be ready to have a child together!

During an interview with Daily Pop, JWoww said she and Zack were finally starting to nail down wedding plans.

“We’re actually finally discussing it,” she said. “We feel that COVID has finally shifted, we’re dong mask mandates and we’re getting rid of a lot of quarantining.”

The mother of two added that she hopes she can have a destination wedding because that would be her dream.

Zack proposed on top of the Empire State Building on JWoww’s 36th birthday, February 27. As for her 37th birthday, she explained that she had a rather kid-friendly party as opposed to her usual Jersey Shore ways.

“Since I had the kids over the weekend on my actual birthday, it was very kid-friendly,” JWoww told Daily Pop. “We went to Topgolf for the first time. The kids played Angry Birds. We just kept it very fun and light.”

And since she’s been back filming for television, JWoww said she has had to learn how to balance working and parenting.

“It was just trying to find that balance, I bring the kids on vacation now,” she added.

The MTV production crew has been very accommodating for her family as well. They allow them to do homeschooling and virtual school while she is on camera.
