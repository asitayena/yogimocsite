Narges Mohammadi Career

Narges Mohammadi Career: She graduated from Imam Khomeini International University with a degree in physics before starting her profession as an engineer. Mohammadi was once more accused of "acting against the national security, membership in the DHRC, and propaganda against the regime" in July 2011 and was subsequently found guilty.
