The Rise of Luc Picard

The streets of Lachine, Quebec, were perhaps unaware of the star in their midst when Luc Picard stepped into the world.

A seedling in the vast expanse of the acting universe, Luc’s roots took hold at the Conservatoire d’art dramatique de Montréal, setting the stage for a journey unlike any other.

The Quebec Darling

It didn’t take long for Luc to become a household name in Quebec. His charismatic presence on television served as the perfect appetizer for the main course that was to follow—his illustrious film career.

From his debut in Letters of Transit to his hair-raising performance as a psychotic cult leader in Savage Messiah, Luc’s portfolio began to resemble a treasure trove of cinematic gems.

Director’s Chair Adventures

Not one to rest on his laurels, Luc ventured behind the camera, directing heartwarming tales like Babine and thrilling narratives in Confessions of a Hitman.

FAQs about Luc Picard

When and where was Luc Picard born?

He was born on September 24, 1961, in Lachine, Quebec, Canada.

What was his early life like?

Raised in a working-class neighborhood in Montreal, Picard was the youngest of four boys. His father worked for Northern Electric, and the family moved to Laval when Picard was 12.

How did he begin his acting career?

Picard initially studied administration before attending the Conservatoire d’art dramatique de Montréal in 1985. He completed his education in 1988 and began working with renowned theater directors in Montreal.

What are some notable theater productions featuring Picard?

Picard has starred in various plays including Signer, Le jeune homme en colère, Le prince travesti, Caligula, and Un simple soldat, showcasing his talent in both classical and contemporary repertory.

What was Picard’s first film role?

His first film role was in Les Saufs-conduits (1990) by Manon Briand, for which he won the Prix Luce-Guilbault.

Can you name some of Luc Picard’s prominent film roles?

Picard had major roles in Cap Tourmente, Octobre, Le Dernier souffle, La femme qui boit, and 15 février 1839. He received critical acclaim for these performances, including several awards.

What television series has he appeared in?

He has appeared in numerous series including Omerta, L’Ombre de l’épervier, Chartrand et Simonne, and District 31.

What awards has he won?

Picard has received several awards including a Gémeau for best male actor, a Metro Star viewers’ award, the 2002 Jutra for best actor, and the Genie for best male actor.

Has he directed films as well?

Yes, he wrote, directed, and starred in L’audition (2005). He also directed and starred in Babine (2009).

Does Luc Picard have any children?

Yes, he has one child.
