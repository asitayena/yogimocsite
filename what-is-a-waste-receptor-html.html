When it comes to plumbing fixtures

an indirect liquid waste receptor is a plumbing fixture that is specifically designed to collect and dispose of liquid waste generated by other plumbing fixtures, plumbing equipment, or appliances that are required to discharge to the drainage system through a physical air gap or listed air gap unit.

What exactly is an indirect waste pipe in this context?

Waste pipe that does not connect to the drainage system directly, but rather discharges into the drainage system through an air break or air gap into a trap, fixture, receptor, or interceptor is referred to as an indirect waste pipe.
