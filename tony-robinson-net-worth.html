FAQs about Tony Robinson

Who is Tony Robinson?

He is a versatile English actor, comedian, author, presenter, and political activist, best known for his role as Baldrick in the BBC television series Blackadder and for hosting historical documentaries like Channel 4’s Time Team and The Worst Jobs in History.

What are his notable early career moments?

Before his fame with Blackadder, he appeared in children’s educational programs like Sam on Boffs’ Island and was a presenter on Play Away. He also had roles in Horizon documentary Joey and the film Brannigan alongside John Wayne.

What made he famous?

Robinson gained prominence for his role as Baldrick in Blackadder, where his character is known for the catchphrase I have a cunning plan. His portrayal of Baldrick showcased his comedic talent, making the character and the series iconic in British television.

What did he do after Blackadder?

After Blackadder, Robinson continued his acting career with roles in series like Maid Marian and Her Merry Men, where he was also the creator and played the Sheriff of Nottingham. He also voiced characters in animations and narrated several documentaries and audiobooks, including abridged versions of Terry Pratchett’s Discworld novels.
