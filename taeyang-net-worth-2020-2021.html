Early life

They are the best-selling boy band around the world. When he first came into the music world, he was a passionate pianist, and he enjoyed classical music that was composed by Franz Schubert and Ludwig Van Beethoven. There was a time when his family was struggling financially, so Taeyang took a break a took a job to support them.

While he was in sixth grade, Taeyang auditioned for the part, Mini Sean for Jinusean’s music video and he was successful in getting the role. It was then he got fascinated by the world of hip-hop music. Taeyang met with the CEO of the record label, YG Entertainment, and he requested him to get a chance of becoming a singer. When he did not get a response, Taeyang personally went to the office.

He finally got the post of a trainee in that record label after he gave an audition. It was here that Taeyang met with his future bandmate, Kwon Ji-Yong. At first, both Taeyang and Kwon cleaned the studio. It was decided that Kwon and Taeyang were supposed to debut as the duo known as, GDYB but the plan got modified.
