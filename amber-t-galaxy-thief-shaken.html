Amber T &amp; Galaxy Thief come together for emotionally charged &quot;Shaken&quot;

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  213 words  |  Patria Henriques

Amber T had quite a remarkable 2020 with a string of releases that saw her grow as an artist professionally and personally, so it is no surprise her newest single continues to showcase how much she has grown as an artist and storyteller.

Working in collaboration with South Coast indie/rock 4 piece Galaxy Thief (consisting of George Bowerman, Ben Watton, Jamie Eldridge and Jake Trim) on the new offering “Shaken“, which came about during lock down and is a reflection of the creativity and dedication to keep working despite the cancellation of gigs and performing in 2020.

Coming together to share their ideas for via Zoom, Amber and the Galaxy Thief were able to overcome the obstacles of long distance writing with their unstoppable creative talent; the result, after much writing, disagreements, laughter and banter was the brilliance.

At just 17 years old, Amber T has continued to show why she is one of the best new faces within the music scene, and definitely one to keep an eye on. Stay tuned for more in 2021!
