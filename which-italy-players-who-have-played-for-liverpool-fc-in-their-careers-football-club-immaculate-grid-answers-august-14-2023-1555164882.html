    Liverpool F.C

    Liverpool F.C., often referred to simply as Liverpool or the Reds, is a professional football club based in Liverpool, England. Founded on March 15, 1892, the club has a rich history and is one of the most successful and iconic football clubs in the world.

    Liverpool's home matches are played at Anfield, a historic stadium with a seating capacity of over 53,000 spectators. The club's colors are predominantly red, and their emblem features the iconic Liver Bird.
