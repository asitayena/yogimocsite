Spencer Grammer Welcomes Son Emmett Emmanual!

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  110 words  |  Patria Henriques

Spencer Grammer had a feeling she would give birth past her due date — and she was right!

The Greek star, who turned 28 Sunday, welcomed son Emmett Emmanual on Monday, her rep confirms to Us Weekly. Born two days late, the newborn weighed 7 lbs., 7 oz.

This is the first child for Grammer and husband James Hesketh, whom she wed in February. The actress recently told Us that her father, Kelsey, was excited about becoming a first-time grandfather.
