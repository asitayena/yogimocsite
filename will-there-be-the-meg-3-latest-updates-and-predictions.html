The Meg Series Overview

The Meg series is a franchise of giant shark movies based on the novels of the same name by Steven Alten. The first movie, The Meg, was released in 2018 and starred Jason Statham, Li Bingbing, and Rainn Wilson. It follows a group of scientists and a deep-sea diver who encounter a prehistoric megalodon shark while on a mission to explore the Marianas Trench.

The movie was a box office success, grossing over $530 million worldwide. Due to its popularity, a sequel was greenlit, titled The Meg 2: The Trench. The second movie was released in 2023 and starred Ruby Rose, Winston Duke, and Jessica McNamee. It follows a new team of scientists and divers who explore a deeper part of the trench and encounter even more dangerous creatures, including a giant octopus.

The release of The Meg 2 sparked discussions about a potential third movie, The Meg 3. However, as of September 2023, Warner Bros. has yet to confirm the production of the third installment. Fans are eagerly waiting for news about the future of the franchise, with many speculating about the possible plot and cast.

Overall, The Meg series has been a commercial success and has gained a dedicated fanbase. Its unique premise of giant sharks and deep-sea exploration has captivated audiences and left them wanting more.
